Bus Spot

Bus Spot is a transit scheduling app makes it easy for riders to visually locate their bus and reduce unexpected runs to the bus stop.

sustain

Sustain is the first iteration of an app that will gameify sustainability for touring musicians and their fans. The app will engage fans by having them commit to sustainable activies, document those experiences and get points for it. In the end, each fan has gained points to get merchandise as a perk for participating as well as help the artist reduce their overall footprint on tour. By gameifying sustainability, it can make some of these practices habitual for each 'player'.

co2mmute calculator

Co2mmute calculator is a simple way for bike commuters to keep track of their milage and the positive impact they are having each time they decide to pedal vs drive. This app will show the positive impact that bike commuting can have on our communities by comparing how much co2 they divert compared to a prius, average size car and an SUV. The ability to put a number and impact on their commute may encourage them to commute more as well as others to start.
