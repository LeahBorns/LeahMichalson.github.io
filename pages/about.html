A little about me

A work in progress....

Design has been a part of my life the moment I was born.

I always loved going to work with my dad as a kid. As a graphic designer his office had every design tool to make a young imagination fly. A rack of markers every color of the rainbow, stacks of stock photography magazines, rulers, light boxes, a copy machine, you name it, it was fair game. I remember admiring his work around the office and using it as inspiration for my own creations.

Eventually with elements of design in my back pocket, I found my passion for the environment which led me to pursue a degree in the sciences. I was always drawn to the aspects of my work that focused on research, design and communication. I designed a whole program to remove an invasive plant species, gathered thousands of data points and created a detailed manual for volunteers involved in the project. I found the most joy in these projects.

The dance eventually brought me back to my Dad’s office, this time working there, organizing databases and assisting with web design.
